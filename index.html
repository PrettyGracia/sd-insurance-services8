Marine Insurance

SD Insurance's Marine Insurance is essential for businesses involved in marine operations. This insurance protects your marine assets, including vessels, cargo, and equipment, against damage, theft, and liability. Whether you are a shipping company, a marine contractor, or a cargo owner, our Marine Insurance provides the coverage you need to ensure the smooth operation of your marine activities.

Key Features of Marine Insurance

  • Hull and Machinery Coverage: Protection against damage to your vessels and their machinery caused by accidents, fire, theft, and other risks.
  • Cargo Insurance: Coverage for the loss or damage of cargo during transit by sea, air, or land.
  • Third-Party Liability: Coverage for legal liability arising from injury or damage caused to third parties by your marine operations.
  • Protection and Indemnity (P&I): Coverage for a wide range of liabilities, including crew injuries, pollution, and wreck removal.
  • Transit Cover: Protection for your marine assets while they are being transported between locations.
  • 24/7 Claims Support: Access to a dedicated claims team available around the clock to assist you in the event of an incident.
